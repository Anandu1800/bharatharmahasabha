Welcome to Bharatar Baha Sabha

Bharatar Baha Sabha is a vibrant community dedicated to the Paravan caste in Kerala, fostering unity and support among its members. Join us as we celebrate our cultural heritage and build connections through various activities like boating, outings, and community gatherings.

Explore our website to learn more about our events, membership benefits, and volunteer opportunities. Together, we can create a prosperous future for the Paravan community.

About Us

Bharatar Baha Sabha is more than just a society; it's a community where the Paravan caste in Kerala comes together to celebrate its rich cultural heritage and support one another. Founded with the vision of fostering unity and empowerment, our society offers a platform for members to connect, share experiences, and participate in meaningful activities.

We organize various events, from cultural celebrations to recreational outings, to ensure that every member finds a place to belong and thrive. Through our initiatives, we aim to preserve our traditions while adapting to the changing needs of our community.

Join us in our mission to strengthen families, empower individuals, and build a brighter future for the Paravan community. Together, we can make a difference and create lasting bonds that enrich our lives.
